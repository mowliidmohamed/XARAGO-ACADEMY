The XARAGO Legacy

XARAGO Fashion Academy began as a small atelier in Hargeisa with a single mission: to prove that Somali craftsmanship could compete on the global stage.

The Foundation

Our founder realized that while the talent in the region was immense, the access to modern tools like 3D CAD was limited.

The Digital Evolution

In 2022, we became the first academy to integrate CLO 3D software.
