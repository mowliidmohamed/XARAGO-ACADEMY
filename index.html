Our Heritage & Vision

The Art of Precision

At XARAGO, we bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and the digital future of fashion.

Founded in the heart of Hargeisa, our academy is a sanctuary for designers who seek to master the architecture of clothing. From the first sketch to the final 3D prototype, we provide the tools to turn a creative vision into a global brand.

The XARAGO Legacy

XARAGO Fashion Academy began as a small atelier in Hargeisa with a single mission: to prove that Somali craftsmanship could compete on the global stage.

The Foundation

Our founder realized that while the talent in the region was immense, the access to modern tools like 3D CAD was limited.

The Digital Evolution

In 2022, we became the first academy to integrate CLO 3D software.

Today & Beyond

We remain committed to the stitch, the silhouette, and the student.
